Multiple motion-controlled games where the player’s physical movement (detected via camera) controls characters in a 3D environment.

Frontend built with **React**, **TypeScript**, **Three.js**, **Transformers.js** (YOLO and MediaPipe pose detection), and **WebGPU**.

Backend built with **Python** and **WebRTC**
