Multiple motion-controlled games where the player’s physical movement (detected via camera) controls characters in a 3D environment. Built with **React**, **TypeScript**, **Three.js**, **Transformers.js** (YOLO pose detection), and **WebGPU**.

**Vision/Therapy Relevance:** A direct prototype for active neurovisual rehabilitation. It combines real-time computer vision with spatial awareness and full-body coordination, encouraging patients to engage physically with digital stimuli.
