A Rust simulation of a jigsaw puzzle solver that models pieces by matching side identifiers, joins them into polyominos, and exposes trace snapshots for each solver step. The project includes an **Iced** trace viewer that runs in the browser via **WASM** and **Trunk**, with configurable puzzle dimensions, solver strategies, image upload, stepping, and autoplay.
